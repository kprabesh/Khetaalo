When?

We offer internships as Summer Session and as Winter Session. Please select the session you are interested in while filling out your application. You also have an option to apply for both sessions.

While on a session, you can also request to continue your internship to next session.

Process

After you submit your application, you will be matched with a farmer/mentor. If you have opted for Homestay, you will be living with your farmer/mentor's family. As a memeber of your host family, you will follow their meal-plans. All Nepalese families usually have 3 meals a day(Breakfast+Lunch+Dinner+ *Some families do afternoon tea with snacks*).

We will help you match with your acomodations if you do not opt for Homestay. You can also make you own arrangements.

Work Structure

खेतालो translates to a helping hand and you will shadow your mentor/farmer to first learn Nepali Farming Practices and later help them on the field.

Depending on the session you sign up for, you could be experiencing Paddy Festival (Mud Festival where we plant rice, sing songs, and eat beaten rice with curd and pickled potatoes), Reaping Festival(Where you use sickles to cut rice plant, and beat the plant to get rice). Work could involve plowing field with spades, directing watering canals, planting crops, reaping crops, loading/unloading organic fertilizers to the field, Fresh Water-Fishing.

Work environment is lax and jovial. Farmers love what they do and will take frequent breaks in between to sit at the edge of the field and share their story, hum their favourite tune, or munch on a snack.

Logistics?

Volunteers can choose work during Summer or Winter sessions. We accept applications on rolling basis. So, until all position are filled we would encourage you to apply.

Travel is covered for volunteers.
Pokhara, Syangja, Siraha, Argakhanchi are the locations available and you can specify your preference while complleting the application form.
Volunteers can switch location as they fit. If in case you decide to do so, your travel expenses will be covered. While volunteering, all of your other travelling expenses will also be covered but this does not include room+board.
Most volunteers commit 15-20 hrs/week on one location before they relocate but you can create your own commitments as you see fit.
